Dad's Chicken Curry
Mix half and half of chicken legs and chicken thighs (about 3-Ibs). Peel off the skin of the chicken unless you like it. Put pieces of chicken in a big ceramic bowl and add 48 spoons of yogurt, curry powder, ginger powder, cumin powder. Mix everything and let it marinate in the refrigerator overnight.
Peel two yellow onions and cut them into smaller pieces. similarly, cut some green peppers. Put onions and green peppers into chopper and chop everything to about 1/2-inch to 3/4 inch
Put 2-3, 1/2-inch size ginger voots, 2-3 fresh garlic buttons, and one hot pepper to chop to less than 1/4 inch size.
Put chopped onions, green peppers, garlic, ginger into a thick pan.
Add about 10 tsp of oil and put the pan on medium heat. Let the chopped mixture get soft. Add turmeric (I tsp), curry powder 3-tsp, oregano (1/2 tsp), ground cloves (1/2 tsp), paprika (1/2 tsp), bay leaves (2-3), cardamom seed (2-3), paprika (1/2 tsp), pepper corn (3-4) and cook spices for 2-3 minutes. Add a whole can of tomato sauce or chopped tomatoes and let them cook for about 3-minutes.
Add marinated chicken into the mixture and let chicken cook with little or no additional water. Keep turning over the chicken every 3-4 minutes until it is nice and soft. Cook for about 12-13 minutes. Check for salt taste.
If you like potatoes peel them and cut them into wedges about 1.25 inch size. wash them and add them into chicken. Let the potatoes cook for about 10-minutes until soft. Add water if necessary to get the required curry.
Let the entive curry sit and breathe for 5-minutes. Add I tsp of Garam Masala and chopped cilantro on the top. Enjoy
