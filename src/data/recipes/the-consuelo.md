---
category: drinks
author: Sam
ingredients:
  - 1 oz Gin
  - ½ oz Campari
  - ½ oz Lofi Gentian Amaro
  - ¾ oz lemon juice
  - ½ oz simple syrup
  - 2 dashes mint bitters
  - Lemon peel
---

1. Shake the Gin, Campari, Amaro, lemon, simple syrup, and bitters together.
2. Double strain into a coupe glass and garnish with a lemon peel.
